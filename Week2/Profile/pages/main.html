Daniel Storey

Daniel is driven and enthusiastic graduate from the University of Kent. Having spent his university years tinkering with C# and the Unity game engine in his spare time, Daniel decided to angle his final year towards programming and software development. By taking on several modules from the School of Computing while learning OOP principles and C based programming languages in his spare time Daniel has established an expansive development skillset and portfolio that, combined with his warm personality, makes him an excellent addition to any development team.
